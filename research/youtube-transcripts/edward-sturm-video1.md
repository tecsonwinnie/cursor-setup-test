Title: AI Is Flooding SEO… And That’s Why It’s About to Get Easier

Author: Edward Sturm

Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nSLnUsOftLA

## Overview

This video discusses the impact of “vibe coding” (AI-generated websites/pages) on SEO competitiveness and performance.

Core themes:
- AI-generated (“vibe coded”) pages will make SEO less competitive
- AI-built pages may rank but often convert poorly
- Human-led structuring and messaging outperform full AI generation
- AI should be used as an assistant, not the primary creator
- Conversion and engagement matter more than rankings alone


## Workflow / Process

### 1. Vibe Coding Approach (Observed)

Process:
- AI generates entire website/pages with minimal human input
- AI decides:
  - Page structure
  - Content sections
  - Messaging

Tools mentioned:
- Replit
- Astro

Outcome:
- Fast page creation
- Minimal manual effort

---

### 2. Recommended Hybrid Workflow (Human + AI)

Step 1: Manual outline creation
- Write:
  - H1
  - Supporting text under H1
  - Call-to-action
  - H2 sections

Step 2: Review structure
- Check:
  - Benefits coverage
  - Missing elements (FAQ, testimonials, etc.)
- Optionally ask AI:
  - “What are we missing?”

Step 3: Write content manually
- Fill in content under each section

Step 4: Use AI assistively
- Improve wording
- Refine phrasing
- Enhance clarity

Step 5: Add visuals
- Decide image placement
- Use AI to assist with image creation/editing

Step 6: Implement page
- Deploy via vibe-coded infrastructure

Repeat:
- Apply process to key pages:
  - Homepage
  - About page
  - Landing pages

---

### 3. AI-Assisted Content Creation Model

Correct usage:
- AI supports:
  - Editing
  - Refinement
  - Suggestions

Incorrect usage:
- AI creates entire page from scratch

Key distinction:
- Human leads → AI assists  
vs  
- AI leads → Human edits

---

### 4. SEO + Conversion Workflow

Focus areas:

1. Search intent

2. Page structure

- Highlight:
  - Benefits

3. Conversion elements
- CTA placement

- Trust elements

## Key Insights

### 1. Vibe-coded pages may rank but convert poorly
- AI can structure pages for ranking
- Lacks persuasive messaging for users

---

### 2. SEO may become less competitive
- Increase in low-quality AI pages
- Easier to outperform with better content

---

### 3. AI introduces “anchoring bias”

Concept:
- AI-generated content sets initial structure
- Humans only adjust instead of rethinking

Impact:
- Limits creativity
- Reduces effectiveness

---

### 4. Human-first creation improves outcomes
- Better:
  - Messaging
  - Branding
  - Conversion rates

---

### 5. Engagement signals become advantage

Better pages lead to:
- Higher CTR
- Lower pogo-sticking
- Stronger engagement

---

### 6. Conversion > ranking

Goal hierarchy:
1. Convert users
2. Then rank

Poorly converting pages waste:
- Traffic
- Link-building efforts
- Marketing spend

---

### 7. AI is useful only with expertise
- Experts:
  - Use AI for decision support
- Beginners:
  - Get ineffective or irrelevant outputs

---

### 8. Section-by-section creation improves quality
- More coherent structure
- Better alignment with user needs
- Stronger narrative flow

---

### 9. Brand consistency is critical
- Align:
  - Content
  - Outreach
  - Link building
  - Messaging

---

### 10. Topical authority requires focus
- Avoid covering too many topics
- Maintain consistent positioning


## Results / Outcomes

Observed/claimed:

- Fully AI-generated pages:
  - Can rank
  - Lower conversion rates

- Human-guided pages:
  - Better engagement signals
  - Stronger conversions
  - More cohesive branding

- Competitive advantage:
  - Easier to outperform AI-generated competitors


## Limitations / Notes

### 1. AI limitations
- Lacks persuasive thinking
- Produces generic structures
- Cannot fully optimize for conversion

---

### 2. Risk of over-reliance on AI
- Reduced differentiation
- Weak messaging
- Lower performance

---

### 3. Skill dependency
- AI effectiveness depends on:
  - User expertise
  - SEO knowledge

---

### 4. Strategic takeaway
- Use AI as assistant, not architect
- Maintain control over:
  - Structure
  - Messaging
  - Intent

---

### 5. Core principle

- Ranking alone is insufficient  
- Conversion and user experience determine success
