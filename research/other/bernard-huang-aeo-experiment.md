# Bernard Huang — AEO Experiment

Link: https://www.clearscope.io/blog/clearscope-aeo-experiment

---

## Overview

Clearscope designed an internal experiment to test whether a repeatable content process can increase brand mentions in AI-generated answers.

---

## Key Idea

- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) focuses on getting cited in AI responses
- Success is measured by brand mentions, not clicks

---

## Experiment Approach

- Use their own product and content process
- Track how often their brand appears in AI-generated answers
- Document every step, including failures

---

## Key Concepts

- SEO → rankings and traffic  
- AEO → citations and visibility in AI answers  
- Zero-click search is becoming dominant  
- Content must be structured for extractability  

---

## Insights

- Well-structured content improves both SEO and AEO
- AI prefers clear, direct, and extractable answers
- Brand authority and trust signals influence AI citations

---

## Notes

- This is an ongoing experiment
- Focus is on process and measurement, not just results
