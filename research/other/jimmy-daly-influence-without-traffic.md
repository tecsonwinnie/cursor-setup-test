# Jimmy Daly — Influence Without Traffic

Link: https://www.superpath.co/blog/how-to-have-influence-without-traffic-or-readers

---

## Overview

Explains how content can drive influence and brand perception even when it doesn’t generate traffic or clicks.

---

## Core Idea

- We are in a “no-click” or “no-read” era
- Content is no longer primarily consumed on company blogs
- Influence happens across platforms, not just websites

---

## Key Concepts

- Content is not just for traffic → it shapes perception
- “Small b blogging” → writing for a specific audience, not scale
- Distribution matters more than publishing on your own site

---

## Shift in Content Strategy

- Old model:
  - Write blog → rank → get traffic
- New model:
  - Create ideas → distribute across platforms → build influence

Thought leadership has moved to:
- LinkedIn
- Substack
- Podcasts
